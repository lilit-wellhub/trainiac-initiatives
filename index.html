T2 — AI Trainiac for Lower Tiers
⚠ Urgent — 5 weeks to test launch
AI-powered coaching for Bronze/below-Silver subscribers · Strategy in discussion
Trainiac AI — AI-first coaching for every Wellhub member
Replaces $6.32/user PT cost model · Near-zero marginal cost · Democratic access to personalized strength training
PMF gate: ~28/07/2026

The case for change: Current Trainiac costs $6.32/active user — 75% of ~$400K/month in PT payments — with a proven LTV uplift under $1. Competitors (Hevy, Gymnahtics) run at ~$2/user and grow faster. The target is ≤$1/user via AI coaching for 3× the users. Current Trainiac stays live and maintained; this is a parallel, scalable layer targeting plans below Silver.

The product — 6 components: (1) Wellhub SSO — one tap, no new password. (2) Onboarding — minimum signal only; every question must directly affect session 1. (3) AI Coach — generates structured multi-week program, adapts from RPE feedback every session, handles missed sessions/injury/equipment changes instantly. (4) Execution — frictionless logging + real-time cues. (5) Accountability — specific PR recognition, non-judgmental re-engagement. (6) Progress — personal records and training evidence, not motivation content. Plan generated in 30 seconds vs 48-hour PT booking cycle.

PMF gate — all 5 required to open Phase 2: Activation ≥75% (sign up → first workout) · Onboarding completion ~95% · AI call complete ~42% · W1 retention ≥44% · W4 retention ≥24%. Phase 2 scope: how PT network adds value in the new model; how existing base migrates; possible premium PT tier.
